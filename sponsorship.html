Church-Led Refugee Sponsorship
የካናዳ ስደተኞች የግል ስፖንሰርሺፕ (PSR) Private Sponsorship of Refugees in Canada ቤተክርስቲያናችን ከስፖንሰርሺፕ ስምምነት ያዢዎች (SAH) ጋር በመተባበር በውጭ ሀገራት የሚገኙ ስደተኞችን ወደ ካናዳ ለማምጣት ትሰራለች። ይህም ማኅበረሰባችን አዲስ መጤዎችን ለመቀበልና ለማቋቋም የሚያደርገው የተቀደሰ ጥረት ነው። In collaboration with Sponsorship Agreement Holders (SAH), our congregation sponsors refugees from abroad, working directly with the government to facilitate a path to safety and resettlement in Canada.
የ ፲፪ ወራት ቃል ኪዳን The 12-Month Commitment

የፋይናንስ ድጋፍ

Financial Support

ለአንድ ዓመት ሙሉ የምግብ፣ የልብስና የመጓጓዣ ወጪዎችን ለመሸፈን ዋስትና መስጠት።

Providing full financial coverage for basic necessities including food and clothing for the first 12 months.

የመኖሪያ ቤት

Housing & Shelter

አዲስ መጤዎች ሲገቡ አስተማማኝና ምቹ የሆነ የመኖሪያ ቤት ማመቻቸትና ኪራይ መክፈል።

Securing and maintaining safe, permanent housing for the entire duration of the sponsorship period.

ማኅበራዊ አገልግሎት

Social Support

አዲስ መጤዎች በትምህርት፣ በጤና እና በሥራ መስክ እንዲሰማሩ ማገዝና ማበረታታት።

Facilitating emotional and social support, assisting with school enrollment, healthcare, and job searches.

የአሠራር ቅደም ተከተል Step-by-Step Roadmap
1
Pre-Assessment (ቅድመ ግምገማ): The Church committee reviews the refugee's eligibility under the PSR program and the sponsor's financial capacity.
2
Document Collection (ሰነድ መሰብሰብ): Gathering all required identity, refugee status, and background documents from the family abroad.
3
SAH Collaboration (ከ SAH ጋር መስራት): We present the file to our Sponsorship Agreement Holder (SAH) for audit and approval.
4
IRCC Submission (ለካናዳ መንግስት ማስገባት): The SAH submits the formal application to Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).
5
Waiting & Arrival (ጥበቃ እና አቀባበል): After government processing (interviews/medical checks), we prepare for the 12-month resettlement support.
አስፈላጊ ሰነዶች Required Document Checklist

Refugee Documents (ከስደተኛው)

  • UNHCR Recognition: Valid refugee certificate or mandate.
  • Identity: Passports, birth certificates, or national IDs.
  • Family Info: IMM 0008 and Schedule A (Background Declaration).
  • Story: Detailed Narrative of the reasons for flight (Schedule 2).

Sponsor Documents (ከስፖንሰሩ)

  • Settlement Plan: Detailed plan for housing and finance.
  • Financial Proof: Bank statements or T4/Notice of Assessments.
  • Membership Proof: Church membership status/letter.
  • SAH Forms: Specific SAH internal intake forms.
የአሠራር ሂደት
How the Sponsorship Works

ከ SAH ጋር መስራት | Working with an SAH

ቤተክርስቲያናችን እንደ "Constituent Group" (CG) በመሆን ከስፖንሰርሺፕ ስምምነት ያዢዎች (SAH) ጋር በጥምረት ትሰራለች።

As a sponsoring congregation, we work under the umbrella of a Sponsorship Agreement Holder (SAH) to submit applications to Immigration, Refugees and Citizenship Canada (IRCC).

፩. ማመልከቻ (Application): በ SAH በኩል ማመልከቻዎችን ለካናዳ መንግስት እናቀርባለን።

፪. ዝግጅት (Preparation): ስደተኞቹ ከመምጣታቸው በፊት አስፈላጊውን የገንዘብና የቁሳቁስ ዝግጅት እናደርጋለን።

፫. አቀባበል (Arrival): በ ፲፪ ወራት ውስጥ ራሳቸውን እንዲችሉ ሁሉን አቀፍ ድጋፍ እናደርጋለን።
